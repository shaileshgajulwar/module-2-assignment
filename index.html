in 2014 Britain's Ministry of defense projected a global scenario which stated that by 2015 India could become a global military power with the third largest defense expenditure pegged at 654 billion US dollar. As of 2015 India is the world's largest importer of military goods. It has ballistic missiles with a range capable of hitting all of Pakistan and most of China and it has around 90'110 nuclear warheads.
